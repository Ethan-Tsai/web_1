Han Ye Cold Noodles King Korean Resturant

A very distinctive Korean restaurant, located near Gushan Ferry Station, only 5 minutes from Sun Yat-sen University. One of the most distinctive dishes is Korean cold noodles. The cold soup and springy noodles are a good choice for hot summer days. In addition, Korean barbecue is also available there, which is a good place for gatherings and drinks.
Business hours are: 11:30–14:30, 17:00–21:00
*off day: Wednesday, Thursday*
Address: No. 57, Binhai 1st Road, Gushan District, Kaohsiung City 804

Hong Kong Fortune Roast Meat

A delicious Hong Kong-style restaurant, with roast meat rice (siu mei), beef flat rice noodles, Hong Kong Fried Rice (Guangzhou Fried Rice).
And there is always a long queue of people during the meal time every night.
Business hours are: 10:00–20:00
*off day: Saturday*
Address: No. 108, Qixian 3rd Road, Yancheng District, Kaohsiung City 803

He Yu Pavilion

Located in Yancheng District near the Love River, it is a traditional Japanese restaurant.
During meal time, there are often Japanese customers in the restaurant, and Japanese is the main language in the restaurant.
Its Japanese steamed egg, miso soup in the combo set meal is very delicate and delicious, and the main meal is even more amazing. like mackerel, tempura
Business hours are: 11:00–14:00, 17:00–23:00，Wednesday: 17:00–23:00
*off day: Thursday*
Address: No. 30, Wufu 4th Road, Yancheng District, Kaohsiung City 803

Kaohsiung Beigang Cai Tongzai Rice Cake

A traditional Chinese snack bar, awarded Michelin Bib Gournand in 2022.
Located on the banks of the Love River, it has three storefronts wide, and every day before opening for business, there will be queues.
Its most famous item is the rice cake, which is very delicious with its rich flavor and sticky rice texture.
Another famous item is the steamed egg soup, which has a smooth taste and rich soup, which makes people flock to it.
Business hours are: 13:30–21:30
*off day: Wednesday*
Address: No. 57,No. 167, Hexi Road, Yancheng District, Kaohsiung City 803
